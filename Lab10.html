Winter in Toronto

The best way to enjoy winter is to get outside. Check out these 5 fun, outdoor activities to do in Toronto this winter!

Winter begins on Dec. 21, and the Farmers' Almanac has already released predictions over a month before summer ends.

It predicts that winter of 2024-2025 will see below-normal temperatures for about two-thirds of Canada, from east of the Rockies to Ontario.

The Almanac said that La Niña, which is the periodic cooling of sea-surface temperatures across the east-central equatorial Pacific, is expected to develop and hang on through the season.
